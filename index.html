WELCOME TO DUET

Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET) is one of the leading public engineering universities in Bangladesh. It is committed to provide quality education and research for the development of the nation.

ABOUT DUET

Dhaka University of Engineering & Technology (DUET) was established in 1963 as a regional engineering college and later upgraded to a full-fledged university. DUET is known for its quality education, modern facilities and disciplined campus life. We are dedicated to produce skilled, honest and responsible engineers for building a better future.
